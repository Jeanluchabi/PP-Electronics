Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: How can I track my order?

Once your order has been shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number on our website to track your order's status in real-time.

Q2: What is your return policy?

You can return any product within 30 days of purchase. The item must be in its original condition and packaging. Please contact our customer service for more details on the return process.

Q3: Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we offer international shipping to selected countries. Shipping costs and delivery times vary depending on the destination. Please check our shipping policy for more information.

Q4: How can I contact customer support?

You can contact our customer support team via the "Contact Us" page on our website. Alternatively, you can email us at support@ppelectronics.com or call us at +123-456-7890.

Q5: Can I cancel or modify my order?

Orders can be canceled or modified within 24 hours of placing them. After this period, the order may have already been processed, and we might not be able to make changes. Please contact customer service as soon as possible for assistance.
